Key Facts

  • 48 teams from 9 states (Connecticut, Delaware, Florida, Maryland, New Jersey, New York, Pennsylvania, Virginia, Washington) attended, for a total of just over 800 high school students.
    • 12 returning schools / 16 teams of the 36 teams that attended the inaugural 2017 tournament.
    • Average distance of 182.6 miles from Princeton University.
    • Median distance of 60 miles from Princeton University.
  • 128 undergraduate and graduate volunteers from 25 academic departments volunteered.
  • Team of 19 organizers from 8 academic departments planned the tournament.
  • 64 rooms in 11 buildings across campus were used.
  • First college-run invitational tournament in country to waive registration fees.
